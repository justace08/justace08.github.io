About Us

Founded on 1997, Linkwise Technology Pte Ltd has always been focusing on continuous research and development for industry device network, communication and monitoring & control system.

Based in Singapore, Linkwise Technology Pte Ltd is the manufacturer of Picobox, a cutting edge monitoring and control system that are applicable to a broad range of application through out various needs and requirements. Picobox systems are manufactured under stringent control and quality assurance. As a result of continuous determination to develop an avant-garde system in the market, one of the Picobox Systems, Message Master 2000, was awarded with the prestigious Singapore Industrial Automation Award in 2003.

In addition to PICOBOX, Linkwise Technology Pte Ltd also develops MCONEX, a unique development and runtime software that incorporates all the tools necessary to build exceptional Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) HMI applications, dashboards and Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) interfaces; truly a tool to empower people and companies to develop graphical interfaces, integrate web based solutions, and take advantage of Internet connectivity. "Simple solution for a complex world" has always been Linkwise's motto since day one and we're putting our best effort to keep it that way. Providing a comprehensive solution to every monitoring and control need, while at the same time keeping it simple and user accessible.
