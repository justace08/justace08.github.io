How prepared are you for a flash flood?

Last June 23, 2020, Singapore experienced heavy rainfall that causes an unexpected flash flood. According to the Strait Times Singapore, fifteen firefighters from Changi fire station sprang into action to help drivers, passengers, and pedestrians that are caught in flash floods. Other areas in Singapore also reported that a similar situation was experienced.

These are an example of why flood should be avoided. At times like this flood is the last thing you wished to experience next to the fire. It can damage a wide range of communities, thus; it can threaten public health, safety because social distancing and public gathering may not be avoided in case of flood. Also, damage to the environment may cause a loss of business, assets, and even people's lives.

How can we prevent a flood if it’s a natural calamity?

The answer is we can’t prevent a flood from happening but we can come prepared before it happens.

LINKWISE TECHNOLOGY developed a FLOOD MONITORING SYSTEM which monitors water levels in remote sites in rivers, creeks, canals, and roads. These alerts can be extended to the public via a website or thru SMS/Text alerts.
