Why having REX will give you an edge in managing your data center

The COVID-19 outbreak is infecting and affecting millions of people. It is also having a big impact on the global economy. As the coronavirus continues its global spread, business leaders have calculated the implications of this evolving situation and activated their business continuity plans. Some businesses have started implementing measures to encourage social distancing and to lessen human contact by getting employees to work from home and on skeletal shifts.

Many companies around the world have been mandated to close their offices and factories, for their employees to self-isolate at home. This forced companies to embrace technology to facilitate their operations in order to continue their businesses and not come to a standstill. Online collaboration has also been enforced and different tool is being used to carry on their business transactions anytime and anywhere.
