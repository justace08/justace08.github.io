Monitoring your devices and securing its data is critical in managing a facility. Typically, you can access your device data through your private network where the device is installed, which is excellent. However, this kind of set-up, gave a limitation to the users like engineers or business owner that has a limited time visiting the site or the location of the device itself.

Using Linkwise Cloud, it will provide you an interface to monitor and configure information of your equipment and devices, remotely and in real-time. It can send real-time notification thru SMS, Email and Line application. In addition, clients can manage the users and their accessibility with the system. Providing you the ability to check and scan all devices that is connected to your cloud with the help of Cloud Scanner. A hassle-free web application where you can access your equipment status daily with your laptop, desktop, tablet, or phone whenever or wherever you are.
